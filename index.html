#Time for Taiwan

Taiwan is one of the safest places and we have the best public health system in the world. We are also known as the Covid-19 free country at the moment. In 2019, Taiwan was ranked as the Nr.1 place for expats to live. We have a female president, a transgender IT Minister and legal marriage for homosexuals. Taiwan is a democratic and liberal country. We are multicultural and we respect individual differences. Above all that, we have a wide variety of nature sceneries including beach, river, mountain, and valley to offer. Despite not being recognized by most nations and excluded by the world, Taiwanese people are considered to be one of the most friendly people. We are genuinely there to help when you need it.
